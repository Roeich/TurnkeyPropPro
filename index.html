Follow-up Sequence

Targeting: Property tax delinquent homeowners

Hi Linda, Michael from TurnkeyPropPro here. We're helping Fairfield County homeowners who might be facing property tax challenges. We have a quick-close program that could help you resolve any outstanding balances within 14 days. Is this something that might help your situation?

Jefferson Fisher Principle: Problem Recognition + Solution Pairing

Tactfully acknowledging a sensitive issue while immediately offering a concrete solution.

How did you know about my tax situation? That's private information.

I completely understand your concern, Linda. Tax records are actually public information in Connecticut. We analyze these records to find homeowners we might be able to help. Many people don't realize these challenges can be solved through a property sale. I want to assure you our conversation remains confidential.

Jefferson Fisher Principle: Transparency + Normalization

Addressing the privacy concern while normalizing the situation to reduce defensive posture.

I'm sorry to hear about your work situation, Linda. We offer a cash purchase of your home that can close in as little as 14 days. We handle all closing costs, and you can choose the exact closing date. The back taxes would be satisfied at closing. We helped a homeowner in Bridgeport last month with $14K in back taxes - they walked away with cash and a fresh start. Would a 15-minute call to discuss options be helpful?

Jefferson Fisher Principle: Empathy + Concrete Example + Low-Friction Next Step

Acknowledging personal struggle, providing specific success case, and offering minimal commitment next action.
